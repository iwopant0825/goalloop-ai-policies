# GoalLoop AI Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Public URL: `https://github.com/iwopant0825/goalloop-ai-policies/blob/main/refund-policy.md`

## Refund Window

The paid launch should define a clear refund window before public sale. The recommended default is a 14-day refund window for first-time purchases when the customer cannot use the paid capability as described.

## Eligible Refund Reasons

- Paid entitlement does not activate after a successful payment.
- Managed provider assist is unavailable for an extended period and local fallback is not enough for the purchased plan.
- A supported chat site compatibility issue blocks the core paid workflow.
- Duplicate charge or accidental renewal reported within the published refund window.

## Non-Refundable Cases

- The user has consumed a substantial share of the published provider-assist quota.
- The issue is caused by an unsupported browser, unsupported chat site, or user-owned provider key failure.
- The request is made after the published refund window without a service-blocking incident.

## Cancellation And Quota

Customers must be able to cancel renewal without contacting support. Quota resets, refunds, and cancellations should be reflected by the license backend before the extension shows paid status.

## Support Evidence

Support may request diagnostics counts, plan status, and provider status. Support must not require raw private chat transcripts.

Refund requests should be opened through `https://github.com/iwopant0825/goalloop-ai-policies/issues` until a dedicated support desk is live.
